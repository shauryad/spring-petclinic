# Cognition - Devin Demo Exercise

## Objective

You will simulate an internal prep meeting where you present a Devin demo you've prepared for a specific enterprise prospect. Your goal is to show how you would set up, narrate, and position Devin in a real customer demo.

We are looking for:

- Clear customer framing
- Ability to structure a Devin demo
- Understanding of how Devin works at a conceptual level
- Ability to connect technical work to business impact
- Confident demo facilitation and storytelling

This is to test your ability to run a high-quality technical demo.

---

## Step 1 — Choose One of These Use Cases (Pick One)

Each of these works reliably with Devin and is easy to demo on many repos:

1. Upgrade a dependency or framework (e.g., Angular 14 → 18, Java 11 → 17, etc.)
2. Improve or add test coverage to an existing service
3. Refactor or modernize part of an existing codebase (e.g., convert class → functional components)

---

## Step 2 — Choose a Target Customer

Choose a well-known enterprise where this use case is realistic.

Example types: large banks, e-commerce leaders, global manufacturers, telco, healthcare, etc.

- Bank of America
- US Department of Defense
- Facebook
- Expedia
- AT&T
- John Deere
- Walmart
- Accenture
- Pfizer
- Chevron

---

## Step 3 — Choose a Public Repo

Pick any public GitHub repo that:

- Is actively maintained
- Actually builds
- Uses the tech relevant to your selected use case
- Is small–medium sized (so Devin finishes tasks in reasonable time)

---

## Step 4 — Prepare Your Materials

You will create three components:

### A. 2–3 Slides for Customer Context

- The chosen company
- Why the use case matters to them
- Business implications (risk, cost, velocity, reliability)
- How Devin could fit into their workflow

### B. A Recorded or Live Devin Session

Your demo should include:

- Starting the task in Devin
- Watching Devin plan + execute
- Showing key steps it took
- Showing the final completed result (code, diffs, PR, tests, etc.)

### C. A Walkthrough of What Happened

Be ready to explain:

- The task you gave Devin
- What Devin did at each step (high-level, not engineering-deep)
- How this would accelerate the prospect's team
- Dependencies, rollout considerations, and what would happen next

> **NOTE:** While this is a simulated internal prep meeting, the idea is for you to walk through everything you'd plan to show to the customer, so we can see how you'd deliver each of the 3 steps outlined.

---

## Step 5 — Presenting (30 Minutes)

Your presentation should cover:

- Target customer + problem framing
- Why you selected this use case
- Walkthrough of the Devin demo
- What Devin did and why it matters
- How you'd run this in a real customer conversation
